If we add up all the houses with these amounts of noise, we get a distribution of the noise that looks like this:

Most of the residences only have a small amount of noise added, which is enough to protect the privacy examples in this tutorial. A few households have a large amount of noise added, which adds uncertainty for people guessing raw data from the reported data.

As we mentioned earlier, the noise on the positive side counteracts the noise on the negative side, leaving us with accurate overall results.
